PowerGym - Gym Management for Easy Product, Membership, and Schedule Control

This application provides a comprehensive platform for managing gym operations, enabling users to browse products, handle memberships, and track schedules with ease. Administrators can efficiently manage product listings, subscriptions, and class timetables, streamlining the entire gym experience for both members and staff.

Core Features of PowerGym:

  • User Management: Each user has the ability to create and manage their own profile, which includes personal details such as their name, email, and password.
  • Product Management: Users can easily browse the gym's product catalog, view detailed descriptions, attractive images, and availability information. They can also search for specific products and add them to their shopping cart. The online ordering process is simple and secure, offering multiple payment options and allowing users to track their orders.
  • Personalized Subscriptions: We offer a variety of subscription packages tailored to the individual needs of our users. The app clearly presents the available subscriptions, including details on benefits, pricing, and durations. Users can select the plan that best suits their goals and preferences, and easily manage or renew their memberships through the app.
  • Schedule Management: The app enables administrators to manage the gym’s schedules. They can create, edit, or delete time slots for various activities, set specific days and times, and assign activities to each slot. This feature helps organize the gym’s activities and provides users with clear visibility of class schedules and availability.
  • Admin Dashboard: A robust admin interface allows our team to efficiently manage members and gym administration. Admins can handle member profiles, memberships, personal information, and payments. They also have full control over the product catalog, with the ability to add, update, or remove products, as well as manage subscription packages.
